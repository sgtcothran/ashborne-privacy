Delete Your Ashborne Survivors Data

Last updated: May 29, 2026

Ashborne Survivors stores your game progress in two places:

  • On your device — local save file (gear, runes, achievements, currencies, settings)
  • In your platform cloud account — Apple iCloud (iOS) or Google Play Games (Android), only if you signed in to cloud save inside the game

SgtCothran does not store your data on its own servers. We do not have a database with your information. The instructions below describe how to remove your data from your device and from the platform cloud accounts you control.

1. Delete local game data

iOS:

  1. Open the iOS Settings app
  2. Tap General → iPhone Storage
  3. Find Ashborne Survivors in the list
  4. Tap Delete App

Android:

  1. Long-press the Ashborne Survivors icon → tap App info (or open Settings → Apps → Ashborne Survivors)
  2. Tap Storage & cache
  3. Tap Clear storage to wipe local progress, OR tap Uninstall to remove the entire app

2. Delete cloud save data

iOS (iCloud / Game Center):

  1. Open Settings → [your name at top] → iCloud → Manage Account Storage
  2. Tap Ashborne Survivors
  3. Tap Delete Data

Android (Google Play Games):

  1. Open the Google Play Games app
  2. Tap your profile picture → Settings
  3. Tap Delete Play Games account & data → choose Ashborne Survivors

3. Achievements and leaderboards

Achievements and leaderboard scores reported to Apple Game Center or Google Play Games are managed by those platforms. You can reset or delete them through your Apple ID / Google account settings using the same paths above.

4. Need help?

If you cannot complete deletion using the steps above, email bccothran@gmail.com with the subject "Ashborne Data Deletion Request". Include the platform (iOS / Android) and the Apple ID / Google account associated with your saves. We will respond within 30 days and assist with removing any remaining data that SgtCothran has access to (typically: nothing beyond what you can already delete yourself).
