Ashborne Survivors — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SgtCothran ("we", "us") handles your information when you use the mobile game Ashborne Survivors ("the Game") on iOS and Android.

1. Information We Collect

The Game does not require account creation and does not collect personal information directly. Limited information is processed only when you opt in to platform features:

  • Cloud save data — When you sign in to Apple Game Center (iOS) or Google Play Games Services (Android), your game progress (character unlocks, gear, runes, achievements, currencies, settings) is stored to your Apple iCloud or Google Play Games account so it can sync between your devices. We do not access or store this data on our own servers.
  • Achievements and leaderboards — Achievement unlock status and gameplay statistics may be reported to Apple Game Center or Google Play Games Services when you sign in.
  • Diagnostic data — Apple App Store and Google Play may collect crash reports and basic usage metrics on our behalf, subject to their own privacy policies.

2. How We Use Information

Information processed by the Game is used solely to provide and improve gameplay features: syncing your progress across devices, displaying achievements, and diagnosing crashes. We do not sell or share data with advertisers or third-party data brokers.

3. Third-Party Services

The Game integrates the following platform services. Your use of these services is governed by their respective privacy policies:

  • Apple Game Center / iCloud — apple.com/legal/privacy
  • Google Play Games Services — policies.google.com/privacy

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has provided information to us, please contact us and we will delete it.

5. Your Rights

You may stop sharing data with Apple Game Center or Google Play Games Services at any time by signing out within the Game's settings, or by revoking permissions in your device's system settings. To delete cloud save data, sign in to your Apple ID or Google account and remove the Ashborne Survivors save from your account's stored data.

6. Data Retention

Cloud save data is retained for as long as you keep it in your Apple iCloud or Google Play Games account. We do not control retention on those platforms.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by an updated "Effective date" above. Continued use of the Game after a change indicates acceptance of the revised Policy.
